## 🎯 GLOBAL UI/UX IMPROVEMENTS

### 1. Navigation Enhancement
**Current:** Basic nav
**Recommended:**
- **Sticky header** that shrinks on scroll
- **Mega menu** for Services (shows all options)
- **Search functionality** (search projects, services)
- **Breadcrumb navigation** on inner pages
- **Progress indicator** on long pages

### 2. Mobile Optimization
**Critical improvements:**
- **Hamburger menu** with smooth animation
- **Touch-friendly buttons** (min 44x44px)
- **Swipe gestures** for galleries
- **Mobile-optimized video player**
- **Click-to-call** phone numbers
- **WhatsApp quick action button**

### 3. Loading & Performance
**Recommended:**
- **Skeleton screens** while content loads
- **Lazy loading** for images and videos
- **Progressive image loading** (blur-up effect)
- **Optimized video thumbnails**
- **Page transition animations**

### 4. Accessibility Improvements
**Add:**
- **Alt text** for all images
- **ARIA labels** for interactive elements
- **Keyboard navigation** support
- **High contrast mode** option
- **Text size adjuster**
- **Screen reader compatibility**

### 5. Micro-interactions
**Add subtle animations:**
- **Button hover effects** (scale, color change)
- **Card lift on hover**
- **Smooth scroll** to sections
- **Loading spinners** with brand colors
- **Success/Error animations** for forms
- **Parallax effects** on scroll
